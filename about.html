Meet Taaja

My name is Taaja and this is my blog.

I am a graduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park, studying Library Science. My dream is to someday become a Youth and Teen Services Librarian in the public library, but I am open to seeing where my MLIS degree takes me. I love reading and writing about what I read, so I created this blog to share my thoughts about the Young Adult and Children's books I read. I hope that I can be a useful resource to readers searching for their next read and for Librarians who need assistance in curating their Teen and Juvenile collections.
